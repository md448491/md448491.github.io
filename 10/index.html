Life#

Dixie Jane D’Amelio ( / dəˈmɪlioʊ / də-MIL-ee-oh; [2] born August 12, 2001) is an American singer and social media personality known for her videos on TikTok. She is the elder sister of Charli D’Amelio.

Fun facts#

She was born on August 12, 2001. As of 2023, she is 22 years old, making her a prominent figure in the Gen Z cohort. Dixie gained popularity on TikTok. With her entertaining dance videos and engaging content, she quickly rose to fame on the popular video-sharing platform. She is the older sister of fellow TikTok star Charli Damelio.
